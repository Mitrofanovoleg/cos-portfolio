From Ad Metrics to Influencer Analytics: The Wisdom Kaye Report

Published: July 5, 2026

Hi everyone!

For our Monitoring & Web Analytics group task with Markus Fritz, my team picked TikTok creator Wisdom Kaye. We used a tool (from Markus, one of the teachers from this course) called Hypeauditor to make a report about his account: who his audience is, how much people engage with his posts, if his followers are real, and how people feel about his content.

I work in programmatic advertising for 5 years, so I thought this task would be easy for me. I look at audience data and dashboards every week at work. And yes, a lot of it was familiar. Engagement rate is basically the same as CTR in my job. Checking if followers are real is basically the same as checking for bot traffic in a media plan.

But some parts were new for me. In my job, the tools show who clicked on an ad. This report tried to show who is really interested in a creator - not just clicks, but real engagement and reactions per post. It's different for every creator. At first this was confusing, but then I understood the point of the task better. It's not about getting one perfect number. It's about learning to work with numbers that are not perfect and still make a decision you can explain (for example if you need to choose the perfect content creator for your brand).

I think this task is useful for me. For future projects I can choose the perfect group of content creators - for example there is a tool in Google Ads which allows you to run ads on YouTube videos of certain creators.

Thanks to Markus Fritz for sharing this task - now I feel more confident in my field!
